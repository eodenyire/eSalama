# eSalama Schools – Concept Note

## 1. Background

Child safety and timely communication with parents are critical concerns in modern education. Parents often lack real-time visibility on their children’s journey to and from school, while schools require efficient attendance and monitoring systems. eSalama Schools addresses this gap by combining **educational technology, secure tracking, and real-time communication** in a unified system.

---

## 2. Objective

To provide a **comprehensive, secure, and real-time system** that enables:

* Student location tracking
* Attendance monitoring via QR codes
* Live notifications to parents and teachers
* Streamlined reporting and analytics for administrators
* Enhanced safety features including emergency alerts

---

## 3. System Components

1. **Student Application**: Installed on tablets or smartwatches. Sends GPS coordinates every 2 minutes, can generate QR codes, and optionally streams live video.
2. **Gate Scanner Application**: Reads student QR codes at school entry/exit points, validates identity and logs timestamps.
3. **Parent Application**: Receives notifications and optional live streams, can monitor child’s location.
4. **Class Teacher Application**: Receives class attendance alerts, sends notifications to parents.
5. **Backend Administration Portal**: Stores all data, manages users and devices, generates reports, and supports analytics.

---

## 4. Key Features

* Real-time location tracking and live updates
* QR code-based attendance verification
* Event-driven notifications for parents and teachers
* Role-based access control for privacy and security
* Audit logging and compliance adherence
* Reporting and analytics dashboards

---

## 5. Benefits

* **For Parents**: Peace of mind through real-time alerts and monitoring
* **For Teachers**: Streamlined attendance and communication with parents
* **For Administrators**: Centralized monitoring, compliance, and actionable insights
* **For Students**: Safer school commute with instant alerting and accountability

---

## 6. Target Users

* Pre-primary, primary, and secondary school students
* Parents and guardians
* Class teachers and school administrators
* School management and system administrators

---

## 7. Implementation Plan

1. Deploy student devices with pre-installed applications
2. Install gate scanners at school entry and exit points
3. Set up backend infrastructure and administration portal
4. Distribute parent and teacher applications
5. Conduct pilot testing in one or more schools
6. Monitor, evaluate, and refine system performance before full rollout

---

## 8. Expected Outcomes

* Improved student safety and communication with parents
* Accurate, real-time attendance tracking
* Efficient reporting and decision-making for school management
* Scalable system suitable for multiple schools and districts
